# Controller Gesture Audio Player
1 Screen Tap Play Song
2 Key Tap Pause Song
3 Swipe Right Next Song
4 Swipe Left Previous Song
5 Circle (1-2 times) 20% Volume
6 Circle (3 times) 50% Volume
7 Circle (4 times) 80% Volume
8 Circle (5+ times) 100% Volume
9 Swipe Up Mute
